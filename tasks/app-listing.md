# App Store Listing — Shop Chat Agent

## App Name
Shop Chat Agent

## Tagline
AI-powered chat assistant that helps shoppers find products, track orders, and get answers instantly.

## Short Description
Add an intelligent chat widget to your storefront that answers customer questions 24/7. Powered by AI with direct access to your Shopify catalog, Shop Chat Agent helps shoppers search products, check vehicle fitment compatibility, track orders, and learn about your store policies — all through natural conversation. Merchants get a live chat dashboard to monitor conversations, take over from AI when needed, and view real-time customer activity. Customizable bubble color, welcome message, and AI personality. Set support hours, add store knowledge, and collect customer feedback — no coding required.

## Detailed Description

**Help customers find what they need, faster.**

Shop Chat Agent embeds an AI chat widget on your storefront that understands your products and helps shoppers in real time. Customers can search your catalog, ask about compatibility, track orders, and get policy answers — without leaving the page.

**For your customers:**
- Natural language product search across your entire catalog
- Vehicle fitment and compatibility checks with web verification
- Order status tracking with authentication
- Instant answers about returns, shipping, and store policies
- Request a human agent when they need personal help

**For merchants:**
- Live chat dashboard with conversation queue and customer details
- Real-time customer activity tracking (current page, cart contents, viewed products)
- Take over conversations from AI with one click
- Configurable store knowledge (return policy, contact info, custom instructions)
- Support hours scheduling — AI handles requests outside business hours
- Customer feedback (thumbs up/down) on AI responses
- Customizable chat bubble color, welcome message, and AI personality

**Key features:**
- Streams responses in real-time (under 2 seconds to first response)
- Works through Shopify's app proxy — no external scripts needed
- GDPR-compliant with automatic data retention cleanup
- Mobile-responsive chat widget
- No monthly message limits on the free plan
